Saucy Beans

Home

Description

This is a simple recipe for tasty, saucy, black beans.

Let's begin!

Ingredients

These ingredients make 4 servings.

  • 2 Cans Black Beans
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1/4 Cup Ghee / Butter / Olive Oil
  • 3 TBSP Tomato Paste
  • Sea Salt
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper (if you want spicy)

Steps

  1. Strain and rinse your black beans.
  2. Add your fat/oil to a pan on medium heat. Once melted, add your beans.
  3. Stir up your beans and add your crushed tomatoes. Cover the pan, and keep it covered unless you are stirring or adding more ingredients.
  4. In a small bowl, mix your tomato paste and water until smooth.
  5. Mix your spices into that tomato paste solution, leaving out the salt for now.
  6. Pour this mixture all over the beans and tomatoes, stirring it all together.
  7. Cover again and let it simmer on low heat for a few minutes at a time before stirring again.
  8. Salt to taste.
  9. That's it! Enjoy!
